Wolf!Wolf!Wolf!

This piece is tribute to those who have laid down thier lives protecting their country. Posted by Anunay Kumar on January 26, 2019

Let that night be the witness,
Of the sacrifices unspoken and the bloodbath.
Let your howl reverberate through ages,
And convey the message of your majesty.

Let your enemies tremble with fear,
Let your canine mark on their body make them quiver with cold,
For you are nowhere to be tamed,
You are the beast.

Let your pack remind them of the camaraderie,
Of the friendship and the family.
Let your glowing eyes,
Tell the stories of your valour.

Your pack send shivers to lions,
Such is your strength
Let the wolf guard and protect your border,
Let that howl reverberate through ages--
"WOLF!WOLF!WOLF!"
