A girl on a stone-bench.

She wants to live alone.She wants to forget her previous relations and take life head-on.But,she keeps falling back Posted by Anunay Kumar on December 5, 2018

With solitude hovering around her,
There sits a girl, on a stone bench with her overwhelming thoughts,
With an empty heart and an empty stomach:
Seemingly distraught.

The sun has lost out of the horizon
and the dusk has set in.
So is she lost in the memories of her beloved;
Remembering the spring, the youthful summer evenings
Trying to make sense of things happened to her.

Lonely but self-aware-
trying to decipher the bird's hummings.
Finding solace in the grey leaves,
In the deep and dreamless slumber.
Gets awakened by the raucous boys playing football.

Then she looks at the football ground,
The ground was shining and was full of life.
She taps her head and asks-"Whats wrong with me then?"
She smiles and leaves the place merrily-
With her stupid little heart!
Only to repeat the same tomorrow.
