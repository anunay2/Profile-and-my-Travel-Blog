A trip to North-East

Want to meet your creator? Visit foothills of Himalayas! Posted by Anunay Kumar on April 4, 2018

No other country in the world can match the diversity that India presents to you in terms of the culture. Don't believe me? Take a trip would to north-east. Period!


Girls were riding the bicycle on the clayey brown sunbaked road dressed in a light blue shirt and dark blue skirts was a reminiscence of my childhood favourite government promotional advertisement "School Chale ham".It was my most revered sight of the whole trip. Real raw talent resides in the country-side. They are our future. They will run the country. They will be politicians, teachers, change-makers, artists. It is our collective responsibility to groom them to make them men and ladies.


Always high on life(0 point at Bangladesh border)

That abrupt change from The great Indian plains to The Himalayas , that chilly wind, those ice-cream scooped snowy mountains, that river cutting down the rocky terrain, that green-transparent water. All are heavenly!


There was football ground just beside the edge of the mountain under which the river Teesta was making its way down the mountain. The kids were all dressed in a jersey and playing football. It was so pleasing to see that the sports transcends culture, geography, caste and creed and unites us all.


Hamara priye Mitr-Manish in MG marg at Gangtok

When the forces of nature and the human endeavour comes together at the common platform something magical happens. Living on the mountain is never easy. Men made it possible. Evolution made it possible. People and the animals living in these regions have adapted themselves to survive on minimal oxygen. They can handle extremes of weather.


Pack your bags and hit the road. Witness the of the creativity of your creator. Witness what humans can do as a species. Witness the heaven. Visit north-east!
