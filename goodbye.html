GoodBye!

Everyone leaves his home for the first time. Posted by Anunay Kumar on December 14, 2019

On 2'O clock summer afternoon,
in the year 2013, June,
standing secluded on the platform number three,
The boy stood there with his Dad with a bottle of homemade ghee.

The platform was empty and so was I
for I was saying to home -- the first time 'GoodBye'!
The train was scheduled to come at 2:15 pm,
which would take to him to the forthcoming mayhem.

The train came and had a long way to go,
Encountering rivers , bridges , rocks , barrens and whatnot;before it could meet the rainbow.
There were two bags and one big trolley,
which his little shoulders could barely carry.

Daddy put those three bags on his seat ,
and met him one more time for the final greet.
His eyes felt heavy and the tears were hung
But Daddy looked away, for he didn't want to see him cry so young.

The whistle blew long and hard.
Filling the void of his heart with the deafening silence,
The train was also empty and so was I,
for I was saying to home the first and final 'GoodBye'!
