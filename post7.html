Aaakirkar dhoop nikal hi aayi.

When you switch on the fan for the first time after a long winter Posted by Anunay Kumar on February 18, 2020

Naa jaane kitne din se thand ka prakop tha,
Pasu-Pakshi ka jeevan bhi bahot muskil se kat raha tha,
Mausam agdaiyan pe agdaiyan liye jaa raha tha
Char pehar thand ka saaya tha,
Lekin akhirkaar Dhoop nikal hi aayi|

Ladke ab bat ball leke cricket khelne nikal gyein,
Titliyan phool par madraani lagin,
Mehboob apne mehbooba ke saath bahar waqt jaya karne lagein,
Aam ke ped manjar se lad gyein aur apni saundhi khusboo bikherne laga
Kyunki Akhirkar dhoop nikal hi aayi|

Pedo ki daaliyan madmast hawa mei zhoolne lagi,
Hawa ke thapede hotho ke nami nichode lage,
Aaj teen maas baad kamre mei wapis pankhe chalne lage,
Jama hua Naariyal tel bhi ab phigalne laga tha,
Kyunki Akirkar dhoop nikal hi aayi|

En sabhi ke beech mei apne kidki khol apne kamre mei baitha tha,
Halki halki dhoop ka luft utha raha tha,
Aur ess mausam ke khayalo mei khoya tha,
bahar balak rang birange patang uda rahein thein,
Akhirkaar dhoop nikal hi aayi thi|

Aaj jeevan kitna bhi andhere mei kyu na ho,
Badal garaj rahein ho,Sitlehar padh rahi ho,
Sabar Haoushla aur mehnat ka daaman thameiye,
Aur saath saath jeevan ka anand lijiye,
Ek din akhiri mei dhoop toh nikal hi aayegi|
